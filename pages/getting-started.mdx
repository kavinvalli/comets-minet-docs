## Getting Started

### Login to our dashboard
First, you will have to create a new account at [the Comet developer dashboard](https://comets.exun.co).
<Bleed>![Create New Account](/new-account.png)</Bleed>
<Callout>We only support Github Authentication for now. Google Sign In coming up!</Callout>

### Create a new app
Click on [Developer](https://comets.exun.co/developer) at the top right corner of the screen. In the `Apps` section, click on `Create new app`.
Copy the `Api Key`. You will need this later.
